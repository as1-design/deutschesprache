German Verb Memory

Gegensatzpaare · Opposite Pairs

How to play: Print pages 1 & 2 (fronts), then flip the paper and print page 3 (backs). Cut along the card outlines and shuffle. Take turns flipping two cards — match each verb with its opposite to win the pair!

Tip: Print on card stock (200g) and use a paper cutter for clean edges.
